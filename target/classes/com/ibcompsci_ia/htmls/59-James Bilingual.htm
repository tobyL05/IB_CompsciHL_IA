SURAT YAKOBUS

THE EPISTLE OF JAMES

 

 

Penulis: Yakobus, hamba Allah dan Tuhan Yesus Kristus; saudara kandung Tuhan Yesus (1:1; Mat 13:55)

Author: James, a slave of God and of the Lord Jesus Christ; the Lord's flesh brother (1:1; Matt. 13:55).

 

 

Waktu Penulisan: Mungkin sekitar 50 M, sebelum kemerosotan gereja, yang tidak pernah disinggung dalam kitab ini.

Time of Writing: Perhaps around A.D. 50, prior to the decline of the church, which is never alluded to in this book.

 

 

Tempat penulisan: Mungkin Yerusalem. Nampaknya Yakobus tidak pernah meninggalkan tempat ini.

Place of Writing: Probably Jerusalem, from which James apparently never departed.

 

 

Penerima: Kedua belas suku di perantauan (lihat 1:1 dan cat. 4 dan 5).

Recipients: The twelve tribes in dispersion (see 1:1 and notes 3 and 4).

 

 

Pokok: Praktek Kristiani yang Sempurna

Subject of James: Practical Christian Perfection
