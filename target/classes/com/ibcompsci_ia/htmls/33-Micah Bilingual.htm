Penulis: Mikha (1:1).

Author: Micah (1:1).

 

 

Waktu Ministrinya: Sekitar 750-710 SM, pada masa Yesaya dan Hosea.

Time of His Ministry: About 750-710 B.C., at the time of Isaiah and Hosea.

 

 

Tempat Ministrinya: Kerajaan Yehuda di selatan.

Place of His Ministry: The southern kingdom of Judah.

 

 

Sasaran Ministrinya: Seluruh bangsa Israel, termasuk Yehuda.

Object of His Ministry: The whole nation of Israel, including Judah.

 

 

Pokok: Teguran Tuhan dan Pemulihan-Nya atas Israel

Subject of Micah: Jehovah's Reproof on Israel and His Restoration of Israel
