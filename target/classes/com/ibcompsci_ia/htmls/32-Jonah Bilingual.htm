Penulis: Yunus (1:1).

Author: Jonah (1:1).

 

 

Waktu Ministrinya: Sekitar 862 SM, pada masa nabi Elisa (896-838 SM).

Time of His Ministry: About 862 B.C., at the time of the prophet Elisha (896-838 B.C.).

 

 

Tempat Ministrinya: Niniwe, ibu kota Asyur.

Place of His Ministry: Nineveh, the capital of Assyria.

 

 

Sasaran Ministrinya: Orang Niniwe (1:2; 3:2)

Object of His Ministry: The people of Nineveh (1:2; 3:2).

 

 

Pokok: Keselamatan Tuhan bahkan Mencapai Kota Kafir Niniwe

Subject of Jonah: Jehovah's Salvation Reaching Even unto the Gentile City Nineveh
