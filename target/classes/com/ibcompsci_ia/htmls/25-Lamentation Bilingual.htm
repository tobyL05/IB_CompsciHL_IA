LAMENTATIONS

 

 

Penulis: Yeremia (menurut 1:1 dalam Septuagin; cf. juga 2Taw. 35:25).

Author: Jeremiah (according to 1:1 in the Septuagint; cf. also 2 Chron. 35:25).

 

 

 

 

Waktu Penulisan: Tidak lama setelah kehancuran Yerusalem pada 586 SM.

Time of Writing: Shortly after the destruction of Jerusalem in 586 B.C.

 

 

Tempat Penulisan: Yerusalem.

Place of Writing: Jerusalem.

 

 

Pokok: Ekspresi Kesedihan dan Kasih Yeremia terhadap Kota Kudus dan Umat Kudus Allah

Subject of Lamentations: The Expression of Jeremiah's Sorrow and Love over the Holy City and the Holy People of God
