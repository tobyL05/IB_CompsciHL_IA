SURAT PAULUS

THE EPISTLE OF PAUL

KEPADA FILEMON

TO PHILEMON

 

 

Penulis: Rasul Paulus dengan saudara Timotius (ay. 1).

Author: Paul the apostle with Timothy the brother (v. 1).

 

 

Waktu Penulisan: Kira-kira 64 M, tidak lama sebelum Paulus dibebaskan dari pemenjaraannya yang pertama di Roma (ay. 9, 22).

Time of Writing: Approximately A.D. 64, shortly before Paul was released from his first Roman imprisonment (vv. 9, 22).

 

 

Tempat penulisan: Penjara di Roma; lihat ayat-ayat dalam butir sebelumnya.

Place of Writing: A Roman prison; see verses in the previous item.

 

 

Penerima: Filemon, Apfia, Arkhipus, dan gereja yang ada di rumah mereka (ay. 1-2).

Recipients: Philemon, Apphia, Archippus, and the church, which was in their house (vv. 1-2).

 

 

Pokok: Ilustrasi Kesamaan Status Kaum Beriman di dalam Manusia Baru

Subject of Philemon: An Illustration of the Believers' Equal Status in the New Man
