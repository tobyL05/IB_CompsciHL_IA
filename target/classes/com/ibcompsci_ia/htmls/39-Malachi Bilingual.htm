Penulis: Maleakhi (1:1).

Author: Malachi (1:1).

 

 

Waktu Ministrinya: Sekitar 430 SM, pada masa Nehemia.

Time of His Ministry: About 430 B.C., at Nehemiah's time.

 

 

Tempat Ministrinya: Di Yerusalem.

Place of His Ministry: Jerusalem.

 

 

Sasaran Ministrinya: Orang Israel yang telah kembali dari pembuangan.

Object of His Ministry: The Israelites who had returned from captivity.
