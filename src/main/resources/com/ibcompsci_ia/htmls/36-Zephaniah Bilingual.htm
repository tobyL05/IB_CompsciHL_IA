ZEPHANIAH

 

 

Penulis: Zefanya (1:1)

Author: Zephaniah (1:1).

 

 

Waktu Ministrinya: Sekitar 630 SM, pada zaman Habakuk dan Yeremia, tidak lama sebelum permulaan pembuangan di Babel (605 SM).

Time of His Ministry: About 630 B.C., at the time of Habakkuk and Jeremiah, not long before the beginning of the Babylonian captivity (605 B.C.).

 

 

Tempat Ministrinya: Yehuda

Place of His Ministry: Judah.

 

 

Sasaran Ministrinya: Daerah Yehuda dan Yerusalem, ibu kota Yehuda.

Object of His Ministry: The territory of Judah and Jerusalem, the capital of Judah.

 

 

Pokok: Penghakiman Tuhan atas Israel dan atas Bangsa-bangsa dan Penyelamatan-Nya atas Orang Kafir dan Israel

Subject of Zephaniah: Jehovah's Judgment on Israel and on the Nations and His Salvation to the Gentiles and to Israel
